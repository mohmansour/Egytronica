Mission

EGYTRONICA will help to Prevent errors and electronic faults by predicting them by Collecting data continuously for each component of the circuit components ,their status and the efficiency,reporting if any error happened.

Vision

EGYTRONICA aims at facilitating the maintenance of electronic circuits process through regular follow-up by the microcontroller making maintenance engineer or technician in permanent and realistic follow-up by the administrator of the circuit.

About EGYTRONICA

Overheating, Circuit failure , electric instability and inefficient design.These Problems will not be such risk to your devices anymore. Upper Egypt in Action Kicks off Tomorrow


Starting From 10/3/2017 ,IEEE Aswan SB would like to invite you all to attend yet another event full of innovation, Upper Egypt in Action. Upper Egypt in Action is a conference where IEEE showcase projects from Upper Egypt, along with several sessions about marketing, soft, and technical skills, and it's back for a new and exciting third edition!. If you're an upper Egyptian undergrad or a school student with an interesting project that you'd like to show off or get funded, our conference offers you the chance to do both. You Don't have a project to participate with? well, You still can attend the conference as a visitor, and have a chance to see these projects in person, and meet a lot of experts in numerous Engineering fields, and hear their stories and expertise. The conference will also have different workshops and sessions with a lot of brilliant trainers and speakers who will get you up to speed with the latest technologies
